# Raahat Website Design Brainstorm

## Context
Raahat is a Mental Health and Wellness Society at IIT Madras BS in Data Science. The logo features a colorful lotus flower with a meditating figure, using vibrant colors: yellow, orange, pink, purple, cyan, and green.

## Idea 1: Organic Wellness - Biophilic Design Movement

**Design Movement**: Biophilic Design with Organic Minimalism

**Core Principles**:
1. Nature-inspired flowing shapes and organic curves
2. Soft, calming color transitions inspired by sunrise/sunset
3. Breathing whitespace that creates visual tranquility
4. Asymmetric layouts that feel natural, not rigid

**Color Philosophy**: 
Drawing from the lotus logo, use soft gradients that transition like natural light - warm peach to soft lavender, representing the journey from stress to peace. Primary accent: Deep purple (#5D3A7A) for trust and wisdom. Secondary: Soft coral and mint for warmth and freshness.

**Layout Paradigm**: 
Flowing sections with wave-like dividers, asymmetric card placements, content that "breathes" with generous padding. Hero section with diagonal split - image on one side, text flowing organically on the other.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Lotus petal-shaped decorative elements as section dividers
2. Soft blob shapes as background accents
3. Subtle grain texture overlay for warmth

**Interaction Philosophy**: 
Gentle, slow animations that feel like deep breathing - elements fade in softly, hover states bloom like flowers opening. Nothing jarring or sudden.

**Animation**: 
- Entrance: Elements float up gently with 0.6s ease-out
- Hover: Soft scale (1.02) with shadow bloom
- Scroll: Parallax layers at different speeds for depth
- Background: Subtle gradient shifts on scroll

**Typography System**: 
- Display: Playfair Display (elegant, trustworthy)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, modern, readable)
- Hierarchy: Large display text (4-5rem) for impact, generous line-height (1.6-1.8) for readability

## Idea 2: Mindful Tech - Neo-Brutalist Wellness

**Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism meets Mindfulness

**Core Principles**:
1. Bold, unapologetic typography that demands attention
2. Strong borders and defined shapes creating structure
3. Unexpected color pops against neutral backgrounds
4. Playful yet professional - breaking mental health stigma through design

**Color Philosophy**: 
Cream/off-white base (#F5F2EB) with bold accent colors from the lotus - electric purple, vibrant yellow, and teal. The contrast represents breaking through darkness into light. Black borders ground the playfulness.

**Layout Paradigm**: 
Stacked cards with thick borders, overlapping elements that create depth, grid-breaking layouts where elements intentionally "escape" their containers. Hero with bold text taking 70% of viewport.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thick black borders (3-4px) on cards and buttons
2. Offset shadows (solid, not blurred) creating a paper-cutout effect
3. Hand-drawn style icons and illustrations

**Interaction Philosophy**: 
Snappy, confident interactions - elements respond immediately to user input. Hover states that feel tactile, like pressing a physical button. Micro-interactions that bring joy.

**Animation**: 
- Entrance: Slide in from edges with slight bounce
- Hover: Translate offset (2-4px) with shadow shift
- Click: Scale down briefly (0.98) for tactile feedback
- Scroll: Cards stack and unstack as user scrolls

**Typography System**: 
- Display: Space Grotesk (bold, geometric, modern)
- Body: Inter (clean, highly readable)
- Hierarchy: Extra-bold headlines, normal weight body, all-caps for labels

## Idea 3: Serene Gradient - Glassmorphism Sanctuary

**Design Movement**: Glassmorphism with Gradient Mesh Backgrounds

**Core Principles**:
1. Layered transparency creating depth and dimension
2. Soft, dreamy gradients that evoke calm
3. Frosted glass effects suggesting clarity through fog
4. Light and airy feel - like clouds or meditation spaces

**Color Philosophy**: 
Multi-color gradient mesh backgrounds using all lotus colors (yellow, orange, pink, purple, cyan, green) blended softly. Glass cards with white/light backgrounds. The gradient represents the spectrum of emotions, unified into harmony.

**Layout Paradigm**: 
Floating glass cards over gradient backgrounds, centered hero with radial gradient emanating from logo, sections that overlap slightly creating depth. Navigation that floats above content.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Frosted glass cards with backdrop-blur
2. Soft multi-color gradient mesh as backgrounds
3. Glowing accents around interactive elements

**Interaction Philosophy**: 
Ethereal, floating interactions - elements feel weightless. Hover states add glow and lift. The experience should feel like moving through a peaceful dream.

**Animation**: 
- Entrance: Fade in with slight upward float
- Hover: Glow intensifies, card lifts with enhanced shadow
- Background: Slow-moving gradient animation (60s cycle)
- Scroll: Glass cards have subtle parallax against gradient

**Typography System**: 
- Display: Outfit (modern, friendly, rounded)
- Body: Plus Jakarta Sans (warm, approachable)
- Hierarchy: Medium weight headlines, light body text, generous letter-spacing

## Selected Design: Idea 1 - Organic Wellness (Biophilic Design)

This approach best represents Raahat's mission of mental wellness through its:
- Calming, nature-inspired aesthetic that reduces visual stress
- Organic shapes that mirror the lotus logo's flowing petals
- Warm, welcoming color palette that invites users to explore
- Breathing whitespace that embodies the concept of mental peace
